Why don't sites always agree?

Sites use different lunar maths and different window widths — some use 1h minors, some 3h, some 2h. Major windows ≈ moon overhead / underfoot; minors ≈ moonrise / moonset. Major-window centres usually agree to within a few minutes across sources; the windows just get drawn wider or narrower. If a day really matters, cross-check with Solunar.org or The Fishing Website.
